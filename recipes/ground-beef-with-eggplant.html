Ground Beef with Eggplant

Description

Another one-dish wonder!

Ingredients

  • 12 (1/2 inch think) eggplant slices
  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped green pepper
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • 2 cups canned tomato sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups grated cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 300F.
  2. Steam the eggplant slices.
  3. Brown the meat in 2 tablespoons of the oil, stirring occasionally. Cook the onions and green pepper in the remaining oil. Combine the meat and the vegetables in the skillet and stir in the flour, salt, pepper and oregeno. Add the tomato sauce and cook until thickened.
  4. Arrange half the eggplant slices in a shallow, 2-quart buttered baking dish. Spoon them half the meat mixture and half the cheese. Repeat the layers and bake, uncovered, for 30 mintues.
