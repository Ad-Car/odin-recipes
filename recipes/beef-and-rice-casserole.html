Western Beef and Rice Casserole

Description

Cook in one pot; eat from one dish. Delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pitted ripe olives
  • 1 pound ground lean beef
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1 cup sliced celery
  • 1/4 cup chopped green pepper
  • 1 cup rice
  • 2 1/2 cups canned tomatoes
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 or 3 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325F.
  2. Cut the olives into large pieces.
  3. Brown the beef in the oil. Remove the meat from the pan and add the onions, celery, green pepper and rice. Cook, stiring, until light brown.
  4. Add the tomatoes, water, seasonings, meat and olives and bring to a boil. Pour into a 2-quart casserole and cover. Bake 45 minutes to 1 hour.
