Jambalaya

Description

You guessed it ... a taste sensation in one dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken breast
  • Creole spice
  • Chorizo sausage sliced
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 green pepper chopped
  • 2 carrots sliced
  • 1 large onion chopped
  • 1 tin tomatoes
  • 1 tin kidney beans
  • 1 cup rice
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 chicken stock cube

Steps

  1. Set the Instant Pot to saute.
  2. Heat the oil. Add the Chorizo sausage and Creole. Fry for 5 minutes.
  3. Dice the chicken and fry in a separate pan until tender. Remove from the heat.
  4. Add the onions and fry until soft.
  5. Stir in the kidney beans.
  6. Add water, carrots, tin tomatoes and chopped green pepper.
  7. Cover the Instant Pot and simmer for a few minutes.
  8. Add chicken, rice and chicken stock.
  9. In the Instant Pot, pressure cook on high for 15 minutes.
